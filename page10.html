Finance & Revenue Generation in Sport

The course is designed to educate students on financial matters surrounding sport organizations. Various challenges, issues and trends will be discussed; along with financial concepts as they apply to the sport setting. Specifically, we will cover economic impact, revenue generation, financial ratios, and financing sport.
